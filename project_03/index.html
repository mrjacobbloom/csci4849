ScanTastic Toe

What even is this

This is ScanTastic Toe, the single-switch, scanning-tastic version of Tic Tac Toe. Use the space bar to choose a space. Every time you choose a space it gets faster, and when you try to choose an unavailable space it gets EVEN FASTERER!!

The AI is borrowed from Ali Alaa's Tic Tac Toe project on Github. Also, my name is Jacob Bloom for the record.
