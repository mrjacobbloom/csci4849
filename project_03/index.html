ScanTastic Toe

What even is this and why

This is ScanTastic Toe, the scanning-tastic version of Tic Tac Toe! Use the space bar to choose a space. Every time you choose a space it gets faster, and when you try to choose an unavailable space it gets EVEN FASTERER!! Reload to start over.

The AI is borrowed from Ali Alaa's Tic Tac Toe project on Github.
